Step 9 - SATA Cables

 

Plug the sata cables into the hard drives and SSDs and plug it into the SATA ports on the motherboard.

It is recommended you plug your main drive that you will be installing your operating system onto (in this case, the SSD) into socket 1 or 0. This will mean you don't need to play about with the settings to make a different port the main one.

Step 10 - Installing the Graphics Card

 

Click the graphics card into the PCI-Express x16 slot on the motherboard.

Then plug the 6 and 6+2 pin PCI power cable to power the graphics card.

For the wireless card, it is the same step. Just plug the wireless card into the PCI-Express x1 slot on the motherboard.

*If you have the same slot as the graphics card, you can use it for the wireless card as it will work with both the small and long varient*

Final Step! - Installing the Front Panel Connectors

 

Snap the top of the cast on, feeding the wires through the hole. This will be different for other cases.

Plug the switches and led wires into the pins as shown on your motherboard manual

Then plug your usb headers into the usb sockets. These only go one way due to one of the pins being missing

Then plug the HD audio into the HD audio port located at the bottom left of the motherboard. This is the same pins as the USB.headers.
