All you need to know about case fans

Fan Size Chart

Large
200mm or larger

Medium
120mm to 180mm

Small
80mm to 100mm

Micro
60mm or Smaller

Generally speaking, in most cases large fans run slower, quieter and push a decent amount of air. However, two quality medium fans would push more air but with louder sound level and greater power draw. Small fans should only be considered in smaller cases where space is limited or on CPU coolers due to the low performance and loud noise level. Micro fans should only be used when dealing extreme space limitations such as a PC in an Ammo Can. When it comes to fan placement you want air coming in the front and bottom and exiting the back and top. Also try not to "daisy chain" more than 2 fans per Molex connector
