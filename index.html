WHAT IS BUILD A PC?

Build A PC is a website where you can learn how to build your own PC and what the benefits are of building them. You can learn here about the benefits of building a PC rather than buying them directly off the store shelf.
PC building is alot easier than most would think. Even a 5 year old can build a PC. This website will also have recommendations for different builds so you do not have to go to the trouble of researching.
