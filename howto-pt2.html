BuildAPC's Guide to Building Your Own PC.

Step 4 - Installing the Harddrive

 

Install the hard drive into the hard drive sled and then push into the slot in the case

Do this again if you have 2 hard drives

Step 5 - Installing the SSD (Optional)

 

Put the rubber wheels on the SSD and insert into the 2.5 inch drive bay by sliding it in. It should then click into place.

Step 6 - Installing the Power Supply

 

Feed the cables of the power supply through the back hole.

Then insert the powersupply fan side down into the slot.

Then tighten the 4 screws into the front of the power supply.

Step 7 - Installing the Motherboard

 

Screw in the motherboard stand offs into the matching places from the motherboard.

Insert the IO shield into the back of the case. This can be annoying/tricky but it will snap in eventually.

Then grab the motherboard by the heatsink, (Don't worry, it won't break the motherboard!), and insert the motherboard into the matching IO shield holes.

Once fully in, you can screw in the motherboard into the standoffs.

Step 8 - Inserting the cables

 

Insert the back fan connector into the chasis fan header on the motherboard as shown.

Then plug the 20+4 pin motherboard power cable into the motherboard. This requires a bit of force. Don't worry you wont break anything.

Then plug the 4 or 8 pin CPU power cable into the motherboard.

Then plug the 4 pin molex connector into the front fan connector. This can be also plugged into a fan header on the motherboard so you can control the speed but as this motherboard only has one chasis fan. I opted for the front fan to be powered by molex.

Finally, plug the SATA power cables into the hard drive(s) and SSD.
